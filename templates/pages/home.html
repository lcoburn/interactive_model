House Model

In my startup Lanu, we told people in the UK what they were allowed to build onto their houses. We had the idea to map their house and then to add these adjustable extensions that would change colour when they would break the guidelines. This is a simple prototype of the idea.

Interactive extensions for residential housing demo

The below model is interactive. You can turn the model around with your finger or a nouse and you can change the width, depth and height, as the case may be, using a slider. If ts not working turn your phone sideways :)

This is a simple traffic light system. The house is analysed and mapped by us and we add blocks representing extensions. The user can vary the size of extensions. Green suggests a relatively easy development requiring only PD, while orange means Planning Permission should be sought. Red is not allowed. Either the extensions is too high or it goes over the site boundary.
